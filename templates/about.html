About

Maths of Neural Networks Explained (MoNNE) is a tool designed to help students understand the maths behind neural networks.

FAQ

Q: How do I use this tool?

A: Adjust the sliders to create the neural network with your preferred settings, then adjust the input slider at the top to see how the network changes. To see the equations at each step, click on a node or connecting line.

Q: I don't understand the maths. Is there a less confusing mode?

A: A more simple interface is planned to be added in the near future.

Q: I can't see anything when I click the Load Demo button!

A: Some devices may be unable to render the visualiser. Please ensure JavaScript is enabled and that your device supports JavaScript. If any issues occur, please contact me here.

Q: My issue isn't listen here.

A: Please contact me here for any issues / enquiries.
